# 隐私政策 (Privacy Policy)

**最后更新日期：2026年3月28日**

感谢您使用 Cryptic（以下简称“本应用”）。我们非常重视您的隐私。本应用是一款基于纯本地计算的工具类应用程序，致力于为您提供安全的加解密服务。

为了让您了解我们如何处理（或不处理）您的信息，特制定本隐私政策：

### 1. 数据的收集与使用
**本应用不会收集、存储或传输任何用户的个人信息、聊天内容、输入的明文/密文以及密钥。** 
所有的加解密运算完全在您的本地设备上进行，没有任何数据会被发送至我们的服务器或任何第三方服务器。

### 2. 历史记录功能
本应用提供了“保存历史记录”的功能，以便于您随时查阅之前的操作结果。
- 所有的历史记录数据仅保存在您的本地设备中（通过 `UserDefaults` 等本地存储技术）。
- 您可以随时在应用内选择开启或关闭该功能。
- 您可以随时在应用内手动删除单条或多条历史记录，或者设置自动清理的时间（例如10分钟、1天、7天等）。
- 我们无法访问您的这些历史记录。

### 3. 第三方服务与分享
本应用未集成任何第三方的分析（Analytics）或广告（Advertising）SDK。
当您使用应用内的“分享”功能时，数据将交由 iOS 系统的原生分享组件（UIActivityViewController）处理，本应用不会记录您的分享行为或分享对象。

### 4. 政策更新
我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。任何更新都将在此页面上公布，并在顶部修改“最后更新日期”。我们建议您定期查看本页面以了解最新的隐私政策。

### 5. 联系我们
如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问、建议或意见，欢迎通过以下方式与我们联系：
- GitHub Issues: [https://github.com/1781257528/connect](https://github.com/1781257528/connect)

# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 28, 2026**

Thank you for using Cryptic (hereinafter referred to as the "App"). We take your privacy very seriously. This App is a pure local-computation tool application dedicated to providing you with secure encryption and decryption services.

To let you know how we handle (or do not handle) your information, we have formulated this Privacy Policy:

### 1. Data Collection and Use
**This App does NOT collect, store, or transmit any user's personal information, chat content, inputted plaintext/ciphertext, or encryption keys.**
All encryption and decryption operations are performed entirely on your local device. No data is ever sent to our servers or any third-party servers.

### 2. History Feature
This App provides a "Save History" feature to allow you to review previous operation results.
- All history data is saved exclusively on your local device (using local storage technologies such as `UserDefaults`).
- You can choose to enable or disable this feature within the App at any time.
- You can manually delete single or multiple history records within the App at any time, or set an automatic cleanup time (e.g., 10 minutes, 1 day, 7 days, etc.).
- We have no access to your history records.

### 3. Third-Party Services and Sharing
This App does not integrate any third-party Analytics or Advertising SDKs.
When you use the "Share" feature within the App, the data is handled by the iOS native sharing component (UIActivityViewController). This App does not record your sharing behavior or who you share with.

### 4. Policy Updates
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date at the top will be revised. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions, suggestions, or comments about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us via:
